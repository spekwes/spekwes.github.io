Our Platform

A more detailed explanation of our viewpoints

College Tuition

  • In 2016, the government spent $91 billion on college tuition assistance. $37 billion of this amount went to tuition tax credits that disproportionately helped wealthier families. We plan to relocate some of the funding to public colleges for tuition assistance instead.
  • For families earning less than $75,000 yearly, we will provide those earning below the 40th percentile of every state with free public college opportunities.
  • We also plan to expand funding for work-study programs.

Environment

  • We believe a carbon-credit system is the best approach in tackling climate change. The carbon-credit system allows companies to have a non-penalized carbon allowance up to however many credits they have. This being said, they can purchase credits from companies willing to trade, or can continue business while paying fees corresponding to their carbon output.
  • The carbon tax, on the other hand, will be detrimental to the US industry as this newly imposed tax will incentivize companies to relocate to other countries.
  • The carbon-credit system not only avoids this issue, but will provide excess money to fund renewable-energy research. We will work towards a healthier environment without compromising American Industry.

Gun Control

  • Our administration advocates for an increase of resources dedicated to mental health and support. Our platform will bring extensive background checks by extending the Federal Firearm License Ownership requirement.
  • Our administration will prioritize making America a safer place by investigating various points of commerce usually be designated as private. This would allow us to live our lives without fear of mass gun violence, and our platform wouldn’t infringe on anyone's constitutional rights.

Abortion

  • Our administration will provide people with the services they need anywhere in America without encroaching on millions of citizens who don’t support it morally.
  • One of our main priorities is constructing abortion clinics funded by states that already have abortion legalized.
  • Our platform supports abortion outside of the third trimester (exceptions are made if the life of the mother is endangered).

Social Media

  • Large social media firms, such as Facebook, must advertise themselves as publishers if they attempt to hide certain kinds of information or media. Having such a colossal public outreach means that the curation of information can have an incredible impact on the political process in the United States.

Affirmative Action

  • Affirmative action is detrimental under the current administration because it is a negative feedback loop. Minority students that get accepted to particular colleges with less preparation for their new environment tend to fall behind very quickly and become increasingly lost as the instructors and other students advance in the course. The performance of minorities is damaged, consequently solidifying the stereotype of minorities being inherently poor students.
  • Colleges currently only use race-based affirmative action for positive public press. With increased diversity statistics due to accepting unprepared minority students, universities subsequently don’t care about the minorities, but they will line their pockets with student debt. The current student debt total in the U.S. has exceeded $1.5 trillion.
  • Our administration wants to provide more opportunities for qualified applicants that might be economically challenged, allowing for less pressure on minority students and allowing them to perform better.

Terrorism from the Middle East

  • Our administration wants to essentially maintain the status quo within the Middle East. We will maintain Teddy Roosevelt’s big stick policy.
  • Our platform aims to support and condemn events in the Middle East on a case by case basis:
      If a country helps us fight terrorism, we will deliver American aid and support.
      If a country commits acts of terrorism, we will apply retaliating pressure through sanctions and potentially military force.

Immigration from Latin America

  • Our administration will increase the amount of family visas and the amount of refugees taken each year.
  • We will have stronger background checks on all prospective immigrants.
  • We will streamline the immigrant process and approve more L-1 visas.
  • We will reinstate DACA.

China/Hong Kong Protests

  • Our administration will not interfere in the politics of China and Hong Kong. Interference in China will cause more harm than benefit because of the billions of dollars in trade between our country and China.
