Our Platform

  • Environment

    We support the battle against climate change, but feel a severe carbon tax would be detrimental to US industry.

  • College Tuition

    Everyone earning under a certain income has free tuition and others will have heavily reduced tuition.

  • Abortion

    We want to provide people with the services they need anywhere in America without encroaching upon the beliefs of those who do not morally support it.

  • Social Media

    The false advertisement of big social media platforms to the American public as platforms rather than publishers hurts American democracy.

  • Affirmative Action

    We advocate to replace race-based affirmative action with economic-based and merit-based aid.

  • Gun Control

    We support gun ownership and advocate for the increase of resources dedicated to mental health and support.

  • Terrorism From the Middle East

    We don’t want a massive policy change within the Middle East. We aim to maintain a position similar to Teddy Roosevelt’s big stick policy.

  • Immigration from Latin America

    We encourage skilled immigration by granting more family visas and cracking down on illegal immigration.

  • China/Hong Kong Protests

    We still stay out of China and Hong Kong’s political business as we are not in a position to interfere.
